Our Legacy

Hindu Girls Senior Secondary School is a well-known educational institution located on Ambala Road in Kaithal, Haryana. Affiliated with the CISCE board, we provide quality education to girls with excellent infrastructure, dedicated faculty, and a nurturing environment that helps every student grow academically and personally.

Our Mission

"To nurture young women with knowledge, character, and purpose."

Our Vision

"Creating leaders who transform communities through the power of education."

Academic Programs

Comprehensive education structured to nurture growth at every developmental stage of a student's journey.

📚
Class 1 – 5

Primary School

Building a strong foundation with core subjects — Mathematics, Science, Hindi, English, EVS, and Art. Focus on curiosity, creativity, and confidence.

🔭
Class 6 – 8

Middle School

Expanding academic horizons with Science, Mathematics, Social Science, Computer Science, and an introduction to elective subjects and co-curricular activities.

🎓
Class 9 – 12

Senior Secondary

Three specialized streams — Science, Commerce, and Humanities — with board-exam preparation, career counselling, and personality development programmes.

Campus Facilities

📚

Well-Stocked Library

A rich collection of books, reference materials, and periodicals to support academic research and independent reading for all classes.

🔬

Science Laboratories

Modern, fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs that provide hands-on experimental learning aligned with the CISCE curriculum.

🏃

Playground & Outdoor Sports

Spacious outdoor playground with facilities for athletics, kho-kho, kabaddi, volleyball, and other team sports encouraging fitness and teamwork.

♿

Wheelchair Accessible Campus

Our campus is thoughtfully designed with ramps, wide corridors, and accessible facilities ensuring full inclusion for all students and visitors.

🅿️

Accessible Parking

Dedicated, well-organised parking space for parents, staff, and visitors with designated accessible parking bays near the main entrance.

🖥️

Smart Classrooms

Digital boards and interactive learning tools installed across classrooms for an engaging, technology-driven teaching experience.
